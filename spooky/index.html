About Spooky Adventure

'Super Mario Spooky Adventure', simply Spooky Adventure, is a one-world hack of New Super Mario Bros. Wii, based on the Newer framework.
It revolves around a spooky setting, taking place on an island overrun with ghosts, undead, and much more!
