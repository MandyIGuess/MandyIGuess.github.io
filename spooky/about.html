About Spooky Adventure

The Story

"Mario and co. land on a mysterious island shortly after their previous adventure, however it doesn't take long before the princess is missing once more!"

Journey along through a world of unique environments in many different settings! Jump across trees, disturb the dead, treak through the finest pumpkin patch around, travel on a boat through a poisonous lake, tread cautiously across fragile rocks, climb a cliff while having barrels and spike balls hurled at you from above, and even face off against Bowser, who's behind all of this!

Perhaps if you get everything along the way, a much greater reward will be given...
